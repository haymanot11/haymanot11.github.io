Who I Am

I’ve earned an Associate in Cybersecurity, am completing an Associate in Cloud Computing, and am working toward a Bachelor of Applied Science in Cybersecurity. Alongside my studies, I’m preparing for professional certifications like CompTIA A+ to strengthen my skills. I enjoy solving technical challenges, supporting users, and continuously learning combining practical experience with ongoing education to grow as an IT and cybersecurity professional.
